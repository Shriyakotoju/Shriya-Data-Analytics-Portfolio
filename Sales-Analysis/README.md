# Sales Analysis Project

**Description:**  
This project demonstrates **SQL and Excel/Google Sheets analysis** on a sample sales dataset (`superstore_sales.csv`).  
The analysis includes **data aggregation, pivot tables, charts, and KPIs** to provide insights into sales performance.

---

## Skills / Tools

- **SQL:** CREATE TABLE, INSERT, SELECT, SUM, GROUP BY, ORDER BY, LIMIT  
- **Excel / Google Sheets:** Pivot Tables, Charts, KPI summary  
- **Data Analysis:** Revenue, Profit, Monthly trends, Product performance  

---

## Dataset

- CSV file: `superstore_sales.csv`  
- Source: Sample dataset for learning / portfolio purposes  
- Total rows in this project: 30  

---

## Analysis

**Key Insights:**

| KPI                  | Value         | Insight                                           |
|---------------------|---------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Total Revenue        | 10517         | Highest revenue from West region                |
| Total Profit         | 1941          | Furniture most profitable category              |
| Top Product          | Laptop        | Highest sales per sub-category                  |
| Best Month           | 2023-02       | Month with highest total sales                  |

**Analysis includes:**

1. Revenue by Region  
2. Profit by Category  
3. Monthly Sales Trend
